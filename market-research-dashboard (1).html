Risk Assessment & Mitigation

Top 5 Risks with Strategic Countermeasures

Internet Connectivity

80% Prob

Unreliable internet in rural areas and frequent outages could disrupt service usage and customer satisfaction.

Mitigation Strategy
Offline-first architecture with local data storage and automatic sync when connection is restored. All core features work without internet.

Digital Literacy

70% Prob

Low digital literacy among micro-enterprise owners may create onboarding friction and slow adoption rates.

Mitigation Strategy
Comprehensive Tagalog video tutorials, in-person training sessions, dedicated Filipino customer support, and simplified UI with visual guides.

Payment Collection

75% Prob

Limited credit card penetration and cash-based economy could complicate recurring payment collection.

Mitigation Strategy
Integrate with GCash and Maya for mobile wallet payments, offer distributor-based cash collection, and implement flexible payment terms.

Price Sensitivity

85% Prob

Micro-enterprises operate on thin margins and may resist even affordable monthly subscription fees.

Mitigation Strategy
Freemium model with basic features free forever, demonstrate clear ROI through inventory loss prevention and sales analytics, and offer annual prepay discounts.

Competition from Free Tools

90% Prob

Existing free tools (Excel, notebooks, GrowSari) and incumbent habits pose significant switching barriers.

Mitigation Strategy
Integration strategy with GrowSari and other platforms, emphasize time savings and error reduction, and offer migration assistance from spreadsheets.
